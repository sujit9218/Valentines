MEMORIES

Parvati Hill is a hillock in Pune, India. The hillock rises to 2,100 feet above sea level. Atop the hillock is the Parvati Temple, one of the most scenic locations in Pune. The temple is the oldest heritage structure in Pune and was built during the rule of the Peshwa dynasty.

Sarasbaug is a major landmark in the city of Pune in India.The place where the park now stands was once occupied by a small lake. However, the lake dried up and was later developed into Sarasbaug. The whole 25-acre complex is known as Sarasbaug. The Ganesh temple in Saras Baug is also known as Talyatla Ganapati which translates as the Ganapati of the lake.

Wet N Joy Water Park is nestled by the hills of Lonavala and it is popular amongst all age groups. It features 25+ high thrill water rides, slides and other world-class attractions.This water park is a popular among friends and families as the the best weekend getaway from Mumbai and Pune.

Pavana Lake, also known as Pavana Dam Reservoir and Pawna Lake, is a reservoir turned artificial lake in the Indian state of Maharashtra, formed by the Pavana Dam across the Pavana River in Pune district. The reservoir is 25 km from Lonavala and is increasingly getting popular as picnic and camping site for visitors from Pune and Mumbai.

Imagicaa is a 130-acre theme park in Khopoli, India. It is owned by Imagicaaworld Entertainment Ltd. The park has an estimated daily capacity of 15,000 visitors. To date, the park has hosted over 5.2 million visitors. It comprises three parks: Theme Park, Water Park and Snow Park.

Andarban or Kundalika Valley is situated about 70km from Pune in the Mulshi District. The Mulshi district of Pune is known for beautiful nature. Tamhini, Shedani, Girivan, Kailasgad, Ghangad, Tailbaila and many more places are in Mulshi area.
